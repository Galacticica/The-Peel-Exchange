Teaching monkeys (and humans!) about the stock market can be dry and intimidating. Traditional financial education often lacks engagement and real-world practice opportunities without risk. New investors need a safe, fun environment to learn about:
- Stock trading fundamentals
- Market volatility and risk management
- Portfolio diversification

## ğŸ’¡ Solution

The Peel Exchange gamifies stock market education with a quirky banana company theme. Users start with virtual currency and trade shares of various banana companies while responding to humorous market events. The platform provides:

- **Risk-free learning**: Practice trading without real money
- **Real-time market simulation**: Stocks fluctuate and respond to random market events
- **Engaging narrative**: Banana-themed companies and events make learning fun
- **Competitive elements**: Leaderboards motivate users to develop better trading strategies
- **Safety nets**: Emergency loan system teaches financial recovery
